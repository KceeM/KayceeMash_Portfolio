About Me

Hi, I'm Kaycee Chantelle Mashilo, a multidisciplinary creative with a passion for digital art, animation, game creation, 3D modelling, design, fine art and technology.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Arts from the University of the Witwatersrand, where I developed a strong foundation in animation, game development, digital design, interactive media, programming and creative problem-solving. Completing my degree was a challenging yet rewarding journey that strengthened both my technical abilities and creative resilience.

As an emerging professional, I am passionate about continuing to grow my skills, gain more industry experience and contribute to meaningful creative projects. I enjoy exploring the intersection of art and technology, using creativity to bring ideas, stories and digital experiences to life.

I am always eager to learn, collaborate and take on new opportunities that allow me to expand my knowledge while making a positive impact through my work.
